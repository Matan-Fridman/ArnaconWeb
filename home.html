Steps to receive Email product

1. Verify Email
Press 'Verify Email' button and follow the steps to verify your email.
2. Wait For Completion
After completing step 1, wait a few seconds for us to send you the email product.
3. Use Product
The product is ready for calling and receiving calls.

Steps to receive ENS product

1. Verify ENS
Press 'Verify ENS' button and follow the steps to verify your mainnet ENS.
2. Connected Successfully
After completing step 1, your ENS and application are now connected.
3. Add ENS Product
Input an ENS name you own on the mainnet, and press "Add ENS Product" to get the product.
4. Use ENS Product
If the wallet you connected is the owner of this ENS, you will be able to make and receive calls.
